Our Technology

Hall Effect Sensor

The Hall Effect Sensor detects magnetic fields and converts them into electrical signals. It is widely used for proximity sensing, speed detection, and position tracking.

IMU Sensor

The MPU6050 is a 6-axis IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit) with a 3-axis gyroscope and 3-axis accelerometer. It features an integrated Digital Motion Processor for motion tracking.

ESP32

The ESP32 is a powerful microcontroller with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities. It features a dual-core processor and supports PWM, ADC, DAC, I2C, SPI, UART.
